Frequently Asked Questions





Why isn’t Life Cycle available for purchase anymore?

In creating the project I learned just how difficult each step of the process was. From cover design, to marketing, to actually writing and editing, each of step is respectively demanding. I muscled my way through the process half-assedly and ended up creating a botched, half-finished, project. It’s not that I hate the stories within the collection, but, I hate that I rushed myself. The cover is atrocious, the content isn’t properly edited, and it just screams, “I hurried to create this just to have something created!” I don’t want that to be my voice. I wanted to gain insight, I did that, and now i’m moving forward.





What is the next project you have in mind?

I certainly want to continue pursuing the route of the short story. Right now i’m focusing on bettering my craft and learning more about the industry. I can’t say I have a concrete idea of what I want do next. I guess it depends on how I develop.





Where can I read your available work?

Right here! I keep a catalogue of works-in-progress, published shorts, and ramblings of ideas here on my site. You can always follow me on twitter to keep updated. Who knows, maybe a new piece will be published tomorrow!
