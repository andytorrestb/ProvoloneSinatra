Meet the author

So, who is Provolone Sinatra? He is no more than a man. He is an observer and transcriptionist of the human experience who made his debut in a horror anthology titled The Haunted Traveler. What is his goal? Perhaps, he seeks to entertain. To educate, possibly? But, most likely, screaming into the void is simply all he knows. In a minuscule room, illuminated by a smoldering ashtray, Provolone Sinatra scribbles prophecies, parables, essays, and phrases onto whiskey stained parchment. His will to live is deeply seeded in the idea that one day someone may find one of these scraps of paper and make use of its telling.
